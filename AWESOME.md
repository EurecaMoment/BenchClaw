# Awesome BenchClaw Benchmarks

A year-organized index of embodied AI, robotics, navigation, simulation, and multimodal-agent benchmarks tracked by BenchClaw.

The list is generated from `benchmarks.xlsx`. Entries are grouped by the spreadsheet `year` column and kept as benchmark names because the source workbook does not provide per-benchmark URLs.
